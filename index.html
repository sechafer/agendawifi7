III. DISCUSSION ITEMS

Course Curriculum Confirmation:

Paulo Ferreira and Lucas Silva presented the draft curriculum.

Key topics confirmed:

  1. Evolution of Wi-Fi standards from 802.11a/b/g/n to Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be)
  2. Technical focus on PHY (L1) and MAC (L2) layer operations
  3. CSMA/CA medium access method
  4. Specific features of Wi-Fi 6/6E: MU-OFDMA, MU-MIMO, 1024-QAM, BSS Coloring, TWT, WPA3
  5. Specific features of Wi-Fi 7: 4096-QAM, Channel Puncturing, MLO, 320MHz channels

Discussion on practical lab component:

  • Participants will need to bring their own laptops
  • Labs will focus on network configuration, performance testing, and troubleshooting
  • UBIQUITI equipment will be available for hands-on experience

Motion to approve the curriculum as presented.

Moved by: Lucas Silva

Seconded by: Paulo Ferreira

Result: Unanimously approved

Venue and Date Selection:

Proposed dates: April 15-17, 2025 (Tuesday-Thursday)

Time: 1:00 PM - 7:00 PM each day (6 hours daily, 18 hours total)

Venue requirements discussed:

  • Capacity for 30 participants
  • Reliable high-speed internet
  • Sufficient power outlets for all participants
  • Projection capabilities
  • Breakout area for refreshments

Maria Santos reported that NIC.br Training Center is available and meets all requirements.

Motion to approve the dates and venue.

Moved by: Roberto Almeida

Seconded by: Ana Pereira

Result: Unanimously approved

Participant Selection Criteria:

Discussion on target audience:

  • Network administrators
  • IT managers
  • Technical staff from educational and corporate institutions

Prerequisites confirmed:

  1. Strong theoretical and practical knowledge of IPv4 and IPv6 networks
  2. Listen to Podcast Camada 8 #54 on Wi-Fi 7/6/6E
  3. Study Intra Rede video playlist
  4. Bring laptop with Wi-Fi capabilities

Participant limitations:

  • Maximum 2 participants per institution
  • Priority given to institutions that haven't attended previous sessions

Application process:

  • Online form with technical qualification questions
  • Review committee to evaluate applications
  • Deadline for applications: March 25, 2025

Motion to approve the participant selection criteria.

Moved by: Sofia Oliveira

Seconded by: Lucas Silva
